Matthew DeMaria
Owner & Red Seal Plumber

Started as a co-op student in grade 12. Became a licensed plumber in 2013, earning his Red Seal — valid across all of Canada. After years working for other companies, he opened Vita Plumbing in 2019. Vita means life in Italian, which is exactly what the trade has been for him.

A family man with a wife, three kids, and a dog — Matthew brings that same care and honesty to every home he walks into.
